Project CWC


 

A uniquely controlled cyberpunk immersive sim located in a post-space war Germany


 
 

Help C fight for her freedom


in a world brought down by an alien attack and destroyed by mega corporations.


 
 

At the brink of extinction, suddenly, the war ended without any trace of the attackers, the only thing left was their technology. We don't understand it but we use it.


 
 

You play as C:


  • ► 32 years young

  • ► Status: Disposed


She fought for the third biggest mega cooperation FRP, the currently ruling CRA of the german government since year 2197. She wakes up in front of the city gates, the former city plaza, and has only one thing in mind: Revenge.


 

As a former soldier with no one to go to and nothing to lose she will get what she wants, it will cost some lives, maybe her own.



 
 

Mega companys, ruling over the current elected government-party since 2100, selling the technology found after the war.


 
 

Help her get back into the city, find out who sold her out and get her revenge or kill them outright. Maybe help her pick up some cool tech and weapons on the way to then shuff it up the elites asses. Or do nothing of all that. It's your choice how you play the game.


 
 

 
 

Featuring a fully dynamic physics system, a quick-save and quick-load system embraced by design, and an inventor system that l be lets you carry anything not bolted down and utilize it at any moment. The level of creativity and jank has only one limitation, your and C's imagination.


The non-static aim System lets you position the weapon how and where you want to use your environment as efficiently as possible and play the game the way you want.
